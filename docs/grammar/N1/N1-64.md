# ~だろうに

| | |
|---|---|
| **Level** | N1 |
| **Meaning** | even though / despite... |

## Example

仕事で疲れているだろうに、毎日手料理を作ってくれる母に感謝だ。

> I am grateful to my mother who cooks homemade meals every day, even though she must be tired from work.

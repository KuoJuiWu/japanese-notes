# ~なり

| | |
|---|---|
| **Level** | N1 |
| **Meaning** | as soon as... |

## Example

彼女は夫婦仲が悪く、いつも私と会うなり夫の悪口ばかりだ。

> Her relationship with her husband is bad, so as soon as she sees me, she just complains about him.

# ~たつもりはない

| | |
|---|---|
| **Level** | N1 |
| **Meaning** | didn't mean to / have no intention of... |

## Example

あなたのことを批判したつもりはありません。個人の意見を言ったまでです。

> I didn't mean to criticize you. I was just stating my personal opinion.

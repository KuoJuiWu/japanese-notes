    N4 — Elementary Grammar Patterns¶

    Pattern Meaning
    ~し〜し (expressing multiple reasons or repeating similar things)
    ~て/で (a cause or reason)
    〜んです (explanation, reason)
    それで therefore/ that's why
    ~ので because..., ....So
    ~てあげる (doing something for someone else)
    ~てくれる (something is done by someone for me or my family)
    ~てもらう (something is done by someone for me or my family)
    ~てくれませんか/~てくださいませんか/~ていただけませんか Could you...?
    可能形 Verb Potential-form
    命令形 Verb Imperative-form
    禁止形 Verb Prohibitive-form
    仮定形 Verb Conditional-form
    意向形 Verb Volitional-form
    受身形 Verb Passive-form
    使役形 Verb Causative-form
    使役受身形 Verb Causative passive-form
    見える/聞こえる be audible/ be visible
    自動詞/他動詞 Intransitive/Transitive Verb
    ~てある (the state after having done something with a particular purpose)
    ~てしまう (emphasis, regret)
    [Intransitive Verb] ている (the circumstance of remaining effects)
    ~と言う (someone) say...
    ~ところ just about/on the verge of doing something
    まだ〜ている still...
    ~ばかり just finished, something just occurred
    ほうがいい you should...
    ~なくてもいい need not do..., don't have to...
    こ/そ/あ② (to refer to things not visible to the speaker or the hearer)
    ~という意味 it means...
    ~ないで without V-ing
    ~やすい/~にくい easy to.../ hard to
    あとで after, later
    ~てから VS ~あとで Continuous action vs Completed action
    ~までに before..., by...(time limit)
    ~ながら while V-ing
    ~すぎる too much
    [Noun] しか〜ない only [Noun] and no other...
    [Quantifier]で (showing a limit)
    [Noun]で [Noun] express many different meaning
    ~とか ...or (colloquial)
    [Noun]でも ...or something
    [Particle]+は/も (emphasis or comparison)
    [Verb]+の+ [Particle] (の as a nominaliser)
    ~と if..., then
    ~ば if...(hypothetical condition)
    ~たら if, when, after, once
    ~なら if (giving information)
    ~のに even though...
    ~ても even if...
    ~場合は in the event of, in the case that...
    ~ようだ/みたいだ it seems that...
    ~そうだ(様態) looks like..., going to... (describing things)
    ~そうだ(伝聞) they say that...(hearsay)
    ~でしょう it will probably...
    ~と思う I think...
    ~かもしれない may...
    ~はず be supposed to be the case
    ~なさい do... (command)
    ~なければならない must, have to...
    Conversations in 命令形/禁止形 Conversations in Imperative/Prohibitive
    [V意向形]と思う I think I'll [Verb Volitional-form]
    予定 schedule
    ~つもり intent/plan to...
    ~てみる try to do
    ~ておく (making preparations in advance)
    ~ように so that, in order to
    ~ために for..., in order to...
    ~(の)に for...
    ~ようになる to reach the point that/ to come to be that/ to turn into
    ~になる/ことになる has been decided
    ~がする can hear, see, smell
    ~ようにする (making a conscious effort to do something)
    ~(に)する/ことにする turn/make to do, decide to do
    ~から/でつくります made from/of
    ~てくる get..., come to...
    [Question word]~か ...what/when/where/who/how...
    ~かどうか whether it's ...or not
    [Adjective]+さ (an Adjective to turn it into a Noun)
    [Adjective]+[Verb] adjective modifying a verb
    尊敬語(Respectful expressions) To show your respect to particular people's actions
    尊敬語 Respectful expressions
    謙譲語 Humble expressions
    ~てさしあげる/~てくださる/ ~ていただく Receiving, Giving honorific
    [使役形]ていただけませんか Would you please let me do...?

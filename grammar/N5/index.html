    N5 — Beginner Grammar Patterns¶

    Pattern Meaning
    こ/そ/あ/ど demonstrative pronouns
    これ/それ/あれ/どれ this/that/that one over there/which
    この/その/あの [Noun] this/that [Noun]
    ここ/そこ/あそこ/どこ here/there/over there/where
    ~です to be/ it is (polite)
    ~じゃありません not to be/ it is not (polite)
    ~ですか (indicates question: sentence end)
    はい/いいえ yes/no
    そうです (answer) it is/that's right
    あります/います there is..., have...
    Locations at/in a location
    [Noun] がほしいです I want [Noun]
    ~なります become...
    [Place] に行きます/来ます/かえります go/come/back to [Place]
    ~が好き/きらい/上手/下手 like/dislike/be good at/be bad at
    あげます/くれます/もらいます (receiving and giving)
    ~ませんか (inviting someone)
    [Adjective]+[Noun] adjective modifying a noun
    ~は~が [Adjective] (the topic has the characteristic '~が [Adj]')
    〜も also/too
    ~を (object marker)
    [a starting point] を [Verb] leaving/getting off
    [Place] を [motion Verb] moving through a place
    [Place] に/で (location particles)
    [Quantifier]に frequency per period
    の (used in place of a noun)
    [Person/Animal] と with [Person/Animal]
    [Noun] の [Noun] [Noun] + [Noun]
    [Noun] と [Noun] [Noun] and [Noun]
    ~や~ things like... and...
    [Noun]で [Noun] = tool/range/place
    ~から〜まで from...to...
    が/けど but...
    [Verb]こと/の (nominalization)
    ~から because...
    ~ね isn't it/right?
    ~よ (informing the listener of something, or for emphasis)
    Verb Group [dictionary-form] Version G1 (-u, -aru/uru/oru), G2 (-iru/eru), G3 (suru/kuru)
    Verb Group [masu-form] Version G1 (-imasu), G2 (-emasu/imasu), G3 (shimasu/kimasu)
    Verb 辞書形 Verb dictionary-form
    Verb ます形 Verb masu-form
    Verb ない形 Verb nai-form
    Verb て形 Verb te-form
    Verb た形 Verb ta-form
    ~てください/〜ないでください please do.../don't do...
    ~ています (an action in progress) (a condition, habit, not a current action)
    ~てはいけません must not...
    ~てもいいですか may I [Verb]
    ~て (how to join two or more sentences together)
    ~てから after [Verb]/[Verb] and then...
    ~たことがあります have the experience of V-ing
    ~たり〜たり [Verb], [Verb] so on...
    ~ましょうか shall we/I [Verb]
    ~たいです I want to [Verb]
    ~に行きます I'm going (an event)/ to (do)
    ~まえに before...
    ~(こと)ができます can do...
    ~ことです (adding こと to a verb makes a noun)
    どんな [Noun] what kind of...
    どうですか how about, how...
    何(なん/なに) what
    どうやって how...
    どれ/どの [Noun] which...
    もう/まだ already/not yet
    よく/だいたい/たくさん/少し/あまり/ぜんぜん Adverbs of degree/quantity
    Plain style [Verb] Dictionary, nai, ta, nakatta forms
    Plain style [i-Adjective] Present, negative, past, past negative
    Plain style [na-Adjective/Noun] da, ja nai, datta, ja nakatta forms
    Conversations in Plain style Casual conversation tone
    Polite style [Verb] masu, masen, mashita, masen deshita forms
    Polite style [i-Adjective] desu, kunai desu, katta desu, kunakatta desu
    Polite style [na-Adjective]/[Noun] desu, ja arimasen, deshita, ja arimasen deshita
    ~とき when...
    ~でしょう? ...right?
    [Plain style]+[Noun] (Noun modifiers)
    どこ(へ/に)も/何も/だれも not anywhere/not...anything/no one
    どこか/何か/だれか/いつか any-/some-
    Numeral Numbers 1-10
    Month/Date/Day of the week Days and months
    ~時~分 o'clock, minutes
    Expressions of times Time references
    何さい how old...
    ~まい (counter for flat objects)
    ~ほん (counter for long objects)
    ~ひき (counter for small animals)
    ~だい (counter for equipment)
    ~さつ (counter for bound volumes)
    ~人 (counter for people)
    ~こ (counter for smaller items)
    ~つ (generic counter for items)
    ~と〜とどちらが which is more ...than...
    ~でどれがいちばん〜 which is the most...
